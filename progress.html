May 14, 2024

We have strawberries! I've been working on converting the existing prototype to support our new gardening functions, and we're now in the phase called "grayboxing," where we ensure the gameplay features are all present. As you can see, the world will be similar to Minecraft, in that it's made up entirely of cubes (sometimes called voxels). We'll have different styles of cubes (grass, dirt, concrete) so the players will be able to let their imaginations run wild while constructing their garden.

Prototype video: https://www.youtube.com/shorts/lYfL9J-AnwQ

Next on the list is getting the rest of our plants in, and getting our economy up and running. We want to let players feel some sense of progression, and have goals to work towards!
